WombTo18 – Getting Started

Follow the steps below in order. Each step unlocks the next.

1Register User
2Verify Email
3Register Child
4First Login
5Dashboard

Step 1 — Register User Account

POST

POST /auth/register — Create your parent account first.

—
Fill the form and click Register to begin.

Step 2 — Verify Your Email

POST

An OTP will be sent to your email. In test mode the OTP is always 123456.

—
Click "Send OTP" first, then verify.

Step 3 — Register Your Child

POST

POST /registration — Register child details. A unique Registration ID (CHD-XX-…) will be generated.

—
Child registration will generate a unique Registration ID.

Step 4 — First-Time Login (Link Child to Account)

POST

POST /auth/first-login — Links your child's Registration ID to your user account. Requires a fresh OTP.

—
This links your Registration ID to your account. Send OTP first.

Step 5 — All Done! View Dashboard
